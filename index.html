💡 The Indian Cafe Thesis
Solving the unorganized Indian college-canteen market.

Unorganized local tea stalls lack hygiene standards, while international coffee chains charge ₹350+ for basic beverages. CRAVE Desi captures the massive middle market: standardized, hygienic Indian comfort food prepped in a central commissary kitchen to guarantee sub-10 minute table delivery.
